The Origins of Judo, Jujutsu, and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

  • Jujutsu
  • Judo
  • BJJ

Jujutsu (Jiu-Jitsu)

Jujutsu originated in Japan as a battlefield art used by samurai for grappling and joint locks when disarmed.

Judo

Founded by Jigoro Kano in 1882, Judo evolved from Jujutsu with a focus on throws, pins, and submissions.

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ)

BJJ was developed in Brazil from Judo, emphasizing ground fighting, positional control, and submissions.
